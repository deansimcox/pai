Your crew

Dan Baker – Digital Experience Director

Dan brings over 15 years of experience to the team and will work with you to define your vision for the project and key business objectives, ensuring that this thinking is reflected in the strategic direction, experience and design. Dan is a regional board director at Precedent, he holds a BA(Hons) in Architecture and Design from Brighton University, MSc in Virtual Environments from University College London, and a postgraduate qualification in User Interface Design and Evaluation from the Open University.

Your crew

Helen Whattler – Digital Experience Consultant

Helen's background spans visual design and interaction design and will ensure the research and protoyping phases of the project result in a beautiful and useable platform for Perth Airport. Helen has a Bachelor of Arts (Hons) in Graphic Media from the University of the Creative Arts and last year was voted #5 on The Drum’s UK 50 under 30 Women Digital List.

Your crew

Ben Tootell – Digital Experience Consultant

Ben brings over 13 years’ experience to the team and will work with you to create the blueprint for your project following a user-centred design process. Ben has a Bachelor of Arts in Information Technology, majoring in Online and Web-based systems.

Your crew

Joseph Barker - Digital Designer

Joseph works closely with Dan, Helen and Ben to translate the research and protoyping into innovative and functional visual solutions. Joseph has a BA (Hons) in Graphic Design from Central Saint Martin’s College of Art and has written a book about the colour blue, called 'Deep Blue'.

Your crew

Frieda Mason - Digital Designer

Frieda's strength in working closely with UX, front-end and back-end developers ensures her designs translate seamlessly across all devices and channels. Frieda has extensive experience working with Tier 1 Australian brands such as Coles, ANZ and Cotton-on Group. She also holds a BA (Hons) in Multimedia from RMIT University.
